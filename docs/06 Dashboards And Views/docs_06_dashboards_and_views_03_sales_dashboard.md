# PET – Sales & Pipeline Dashboard

## Purpose
Provides visibility into **pipeline quality, conversion efficiency, and pre-sales cost**.

---

## Audience
- Sales Managers
- Sales Leadership

---

## Core KPIs

- Lead → Opportunity Conversion Rate
- Opportunity Win / Loss Rate
- Pre-Sales Hours per Win
- Average Time to Quote

---

## Focus Areas
- Pipeline health over volume
- Cost of selling
- Opportunity classification effectiveness

---

## Default Time Windows
- Month to Date
- Quarter to Date

---

## Drill-Down
- Opportunity-level detail
- Event-based timelines

---
